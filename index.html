What is fun.lol?

A customizable public profile platform.

Users can create a bio page, upload media, play music, connect with friends, join tribes, chat, and play mini-games from a private dashboard.

What users get

Everything for your online identity.

Custom Bio Pages

Create a public profile with your name, handle, bio, location, avatar, links, and view count.

Music & Media Backgrounds

Upload a background image or video, add music, and make visitors enter into the experience.

Friends & Notifications

Send requests, accept friends, manage notifications, and see your friends from the dashboard.

Tribes & Tribe Chats

Create tribes, invite friends, approve join requests, manage members, and chat with your group.

Mini-Games

Play Snake, Click Rush, Wordle, and a Crossy-style game from your private dashboard.

Profile Themes, Cursors & Effects

Choose themes, sparkle effects, animated backgrounds, and clean cursor styles.
